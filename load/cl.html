FAQ

Got questions?

What CAPTCHA types do you support? +

We support 50+ types including reCAPTCHA v2/v3, hCaptcha, Cloudflare Turnstile, FunCaptcha, GeeTest, KeyCaptcha, Capy, image CAPTCHAs, text CAPTCHAs, and more. New types are added regularly.

How fast is the solving time? +

Average solving time is under 2 seconds for most CAPTCHA types. Image CAPTCHAs average 1.2s, and token-based CAPTCHAs (reCAPTCHA, hCaptcha) average 1.5–2.5s depending on difficulty.

Is there a free trial? +

Yes! The Starter plan is free forever with 1,000 solves per month. No credit card required. Pro plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to all features.

What happens if a solve fails? +

Failed solves are automatically retried at no charge. If we can't solve within the timeout, your balance is fully refunded for that request. Our current failure rate is under 0.3%.

How do I integrate with my existing stack? +

We offer official SDKs for Python, Node.js, PHP, Ruby, and Go. We also have drop-in plugins for Playwright, Puppeteer, and Selenium. Most users are fully integrated in under 10 minutes.
